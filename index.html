I am a Master student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Cornell University with focus on Machine Learning, Software Development and Data Science. I'm part of Professor Fenqi You's research group and working on an industrial project for Siemen's Healthineers.

I have 4 years of experience in the Power Systems Industry and I used to work as an Assistant Manager for Asia's largest transmission utility Powergrid Corporation of India. Ltd. I was one of the few selected to commision one of India's first STATCOM's. I also have worked as a design engineer in Tata and have designed over four Air Insulated Substations and two Gas Insulated Substations.

I enjoy learning new things and facing challenges. I have great passion and tenacity for the work I do and I aspire to do work which I love.

Currently, my area of interest are Machine Learning and Data Science. I want to contribute to these either through development or through application.

I'm a voracious reader. Some of my favourite authors are Edgar Allen Poe, Virginia Woolf and Oscar Wilde.
